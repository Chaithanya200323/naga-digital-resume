Touch sensor using aurdino

The prototype smart lighting system demonstrates significant improvements in energy efficiency compared to traditional lighting setups. Users appreciate the convenience of remote control and scheduling features, allowing them to adjust lighting according to their preferences and daily routines. Overall, the system proves to be a cost-effective and user-friendly solution for enhancing home illumination.

Integrating a touch sensor with a lamp brings a new level of convenience and interactivity to everyday lighting. With just a simple touch, you can turn the lamp on or off and adjust its brightness to your liking. This user-friendly feature removes the hassle of fumbling with physical switches or buttons, making the lamp easier and more intuitive to use. Plus, the touch sensor adds a modern, sleek look to the lamp, blending seamlessly into any contemporary decor. This innovative combination of technology and design offers a stylish and practical lighting solution for any room, enhancing both functionality and aesthetic appeal.
